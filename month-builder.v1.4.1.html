What this builder changes

  • Publish imported month(s) live writes the parsed schedule and verification data to Supabase.
  • The imported month becomes the current month for only the channel you publish.
  • Older imported months stay available from the home page.
  • The package download remains as a fallback only. It is no longer the preferred workflow.
  • Notes, tags, and rectangle notes are protected by the shared marks bridge after the channel page loads.
